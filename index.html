Story

Some Cringe Backstory

In a universe where humans rule over demons, an elder demon by the name of Qirin desires to restore the original order of ways where elder demons were once the ones in power. Centuries ago, humans feared the elder demons and their merciless natures. However, upon acquiring assistance from otherworldly beings known as Spectres, humanity had achieved the power they needed to strike elder demons down from their authority. After seething compliance and silent plotting, Qirin becomes aware of a spectre called Mallice. Being indifferent to humans and their relationship to her kind, Qirin manages to convince Mallice to join him in his purpose to revive the status of elder demons. He in turn promises her this same status, and being greedy for power herself, she agrees. Their plan was simple, but uncertain. They are to attempt the creation of a child of elder demon and Spectre blood, of which they will utilize its strengths to overwhelm and exhaust the rule of the humans. For elder demons, birth has been long outlawed. If it wasn't already unlikely of an outcome, the creation of Spectres has been a mystery. They appear to be incapable of reproducing. But there's one benefit to Mallice outside of her greed: she is one of few unexplained individuals of her kind known as "Ixntha," where reproductive organs appear to have developed within her body. After years of efforts, a child is finally carried in Mallice's womb, to which they choose the name "Apple" as refernce to the "forbidden fruit." Following a long pregnancy, the child's birth is far from perfect and results in a grotesque and pathetic mass of flesh and misery. It seems elder demons and Spectres appear to have dismal compatability for reproduction. However, this outcome was expected. Qirin seeks out the elder demon of secrets, Yx. Utilizing their ancient ability, Yx is able to seal away Apple's hideous form as a "secret." This secret is made physical as the body of an elder demon resembling that of a human. This is where Apple finally comes to be, with his true existence of elder demon and Spectre hybridity masked behind the less threatening conflict of outlawed birth of an elder demon. To which breaking this law, Qirin is punished and forced into his own form resembling that of which he hates most: a human. Even in this humanoid form, Qirin continues to research into his child creation, seeking the methods to fulfill his goal and regain supremacy over the humans he's always hated. Yet what Qirin didn't expect; the biggest challenge of his plan would not be the creation of the child itself, but instead the problem of the child's mysterious imperfection alongside its inability to comply with his wishes. With Mallice suddenly disappearing from Qirin's side, and Apple being taken from him by opposing elder demons, Qirin had more problems than he could dream to count.

Anyways

A couple decades later, Apple now had his own desires and morals, of which clashed with Qirin's. The elder demon's plan was becoming ever the more complicated. How could he convince - or force - Apple to do as he says? How was he to deal with the oncoming suspicion of Apple from the Spectres? Whether he was to know or not is beyond Apple, who now at the age of 26, simply desires to live a meaningless life of night shifts, booze, parties, and hook-ups. Unknown to him is the purpose and importance of his birth. He's too focused on finding that special someone to settle down with.
